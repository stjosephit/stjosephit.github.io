What does the IT Trainee do at saint Jospehs

The IT trainee at saint joseph's is there to deal with all school owned IT issues, this means that an IT trainee is limited to what they are allowed to do on a device brought in from home. While it is an IT trainees job to deal with all issues it does not mean they can do everything themselves, and certain jobs may require escalation to higher IT support.
